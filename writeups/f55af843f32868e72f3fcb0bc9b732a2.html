UIUCTF 2024 — Night

That was quite a pretty night view, can you find where I took it? Flag format: uiuctf{street name, city name} Example: uiuctf{East Green Street, Champaign}

Some words are blurred out to make the challenge harder, hopefully.

Flag format clarification: Use the full type, e.g. Avenue, Street, Road, etc., and include a space between the comma and city name.

We're given this photo of a street at night:

Through a quick reverse image search, we can conjecture that the building in the back is the Prudential Center tower in Boston.

Looking at the area on Google Maps,

we can notice a few things:

  • The tall Four Seasons hotel is farther away than Prudential tower in the image, so we're looking at Prudential from the east.
  • There are train tracks to the left in the photo.
  • There's an overpass in the photo, and the photo is taken from above (eg. another overpass further to the east).
